Concept

Recovering from eating disorders is an incredibly stressful and uncertain journey, riddled with anxiety and questioning oneself. A recovery journey is substantially deeper than just eating and reaching healthy physiological measures, the psychological aspects seldom go away. There are a lot of recovery applications, but they are mostly focused on tracking meals and logging feelings. These recovery applications are missing the important emotional aspect of both pre and post meal related anxiety. We see a need for something physical and more personal that acts as a companion during mealtimes, focusing on reducing anxiety and negative associations to eating. In this project, we have created a device that we hope will culminate these purposes in everyday experiences, with the help of its aesthetic and functionality.
