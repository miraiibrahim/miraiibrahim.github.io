about me

As a computer scientist and UX designer, I am passionate about creating designs that everyone can use. I think technology has the power to change people's lives, but only if it's designed with everyone's needs in mind.
It's not just about making something look cool or writing really efficient code. It's more about understanding the experiences and needs of the people who will use the designs and finding solutions that really work for them.
I find great joy in creating things from scratch, regardless of whether it's designing websites, drawing, or even creating new recipes. There's something fulfilling about taking an idea and turning it into a reality, be it an application or a batch of cupcakes. For me, the process of creation is just as important as the end result, and I always strive to learn and grow through each project I take on.
Let's work together to empower people, connect communities, and break down barriers to access and opportunity.
