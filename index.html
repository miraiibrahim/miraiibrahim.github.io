about me

As a computer scientist and UX designer, I am driven by a passion for accessibility and creating designs that are helpful and inclusive for all people. I firmly believe that technology has the power to transform lives, but only if it is designed with the needs of all users in mind.
It's not just about designing a sleek interface or writing efficient code - it's about understanding the needs and experiences of the people who will use my designs, and creating solutions that are truly empathetic to their needs.
I believe we can create a world where technology and design can work in harmony - empowering people, connecting communities, and breaking down barriers to access and opportunity.
Let's work together to create solutins that truly makes a difference in people's lives.
